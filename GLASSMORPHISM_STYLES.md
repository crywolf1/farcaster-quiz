# Glassmorphism Theme Guide

## Color Palette
- Background: from-blue-50 via-indigo-50 to-purple-50
- Glass cards: backdrop-blur-xl bg-white/40 border border-white/20
- Accent gradients: from-indigo-500 to-purple-500
- Text: gray-800 (primary), gray-600 (secondary)

## Border Radius
- Large cards: rounded-[40px]
- Medium cards: rounded-[32px]
- Buttons: rounded-[28px]
- Small elements: rounded-[20px]

## Shadows
- Main: shadow-2xl
- Hover: hover:shadow-3xl
- Rings: ring-4 ring-white/30

## Effects
- Backdrop blur: backdrop-blur-xl
- Glass opacity: bg-white/40 to bg-white/60
- Border: border border-white/20
- Hover scale: hover:scale-[1.02]
